most of these fixes are handled by html_to_markdown

&#8211;  # sometimes em dashes are not converted
(\s*\r?\n){2,}  # replaces extraneous new lines, replaces groups of 2 or more with a single
(>\n){5}  # fix blockquotes having a ton of empty quoted lines
\*\("  # fix no space between star and parenthesis
\)\*"  # fix no space between closing star and parenthesis
\*\.\*  # replace *.* with ., unknown why this appears
i0.wp.com\/|\?resize=.*ssl=1  # wordpress resized image url. Luckily the direct image can be found by removing the "i0.wp.com" prefix and "?resize=...ssl=1" suffix.
&#8217; # convert weird apostrophe(?) to single quote
&#8220; # fix opening double quote
&#8221; # fix opening double quote
\u00a0 replace with &nbsp;
\n # replace with <br>
