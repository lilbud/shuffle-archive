Recorded in 1979 and released the following year, this was Joey Ramone’s paean to the classic sound of rock and roll and his lament at how modern rock had become so commercialized.

Ironic (and prescient) at the time, it plays sincerely today, when rock and roll is virtually gone from all but the oldies stations.
