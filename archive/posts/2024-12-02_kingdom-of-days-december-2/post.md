**2004:** Bruce joins Joe Grushecky and The Houserockers for a special Flood Aid concert in Pittsburgh, benefiting local residents impacted by Hurricane Ivan. Bruce joins opening act Exit 105 for “[All Along the Watchtower](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/08/13/matr-bruce-springsteen-neil-young-and-exit-105-all-along-the-watchtower/)” before playing a brief acoustic set and finally joining Joe and the Band for their set. Jesse Malin makes a guest appearance on the show-closing “Santa Claus is Comin’ to Town.”
