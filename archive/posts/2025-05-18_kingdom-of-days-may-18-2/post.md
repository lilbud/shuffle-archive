***New for 2025:** Audio for 1999 and 2014; video for 2014 and 2023*

**1966:** Bruce enters a recording studio for the very first time when The Castiles record their first two songs: “[Baby I](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/12/19/roll-of-the-dice-baby-i/)” and “[That’s What You Get](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/11/30/roll-of-the-dice-thats-what-you-get/).” Both are co-written by Bruce and George Theiss. The former will be officially released on *Chapter & Verse* in 2016; the latter is still unreleased but can be heard below.

[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aTeJTkSJMVE](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aTeJTkSJMVE)

[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_1ET0kehXbg](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_1ET0kehXbg)

**1973:** Bruce cancels a scheduled performance at Michigan Technological University to make room for studio time for the Wild and Innocent sessions.

**1988:** Night Two of five in the final U.S. stand for the Tunnel of Love Express Tour. Bruce and the band exit to a “[New York, New York](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2021/06/26/cover-me-new-york-new-york/)” instrumental.

[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fNUAkKLceYQ](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fNUAkKLceYQ)
