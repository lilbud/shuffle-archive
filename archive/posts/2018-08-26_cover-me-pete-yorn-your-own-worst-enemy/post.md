Ten points to Pete Yorn for choosing one of the best tracks (but least played) from Bruce’s *Magic* album: “[Your Own Worst Enemy.](../2020-05-31_roll-of-the-dice-your-own-worst-enemy/post.md)”

Another 10 points for his beautiful guitar work and deeply felt vocals.

Minus 5 for not understanding the song.

Still a great cover, though.
