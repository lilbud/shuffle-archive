
Kaiak is a folk/indie/pop team-up of Swedish musicians Emil Gullhamn and Marcos Ubeda.

Emil and Marcos draw the inspiration for their music from nature, friends, and "really good coffee."

That description lends itself well to their covers, too, because I can totally dig listening to their version of Bruce's "" while taking my morning coffee outside on a lazy weekend morning.
