We’ve featured Emmylou on the blog several times over the years, always spotlighting her beautiful covers of Bruce’s music. Bruce’s music has been an influence on Emmylou’s studio work for decades, however. In fact, her 1985 album was directly inspired by 

*The Ballad of Sally Rose**Nebraska.*

So it’s surprising that this turn-of-the-century track so deep into their careers marks their first studio collaboration, but the wait resulted in a beautiful song.
