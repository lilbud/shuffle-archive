**1970:** Bruce meets Nils Lofgren for the first time at The Matrix in San Francisco, where both Steel Mill (Bruce’s band) and Grin (Nils’ band) are playing.

**1972:** Bruce plays a solo acoustic show at Freehold Township High School. The set list includes his never-released “[No Need](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/roll-of-the-dice-album-by-album/),” and “[Cowboys of the Sea](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2021/09/17/roll-of-the-dice-cowboys-of-the-sea/),” and “[She’s a Woman](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/01/24/roll-of-the-dice-shes-a-woman/).”

**1974:** Bruce cancels a show in Columbus following the firing of original E Street Band drummer Vini Lopez.

**1977:** Ronnie Spector and Flo and Eddie make guest appearances with Bruce and the E Street Band at the Richfield Coliseum. Together, they perform “[Baby, I Love You](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/09/02/meeting-across-the-river-bruce-springsteen-and-ronnie-spector-baby-i-love-you/),” “[Walking in the Rain](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2022/01/13/matr-ronnie-spector-and-the-e-street-band-walking-in-the-rain/),” “[Be My Baby](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/06/01/matr-bruce-springsteen-and-ronnie-spector-be-my-baby/),” and “[Say Goodbye to Hollywood](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/08/27/matr-ronnie-spector-and-the-e-street-band-say-goodbye-to-hollywood/)” (the last of which was recently recorded in the studio with the E Street Band).
