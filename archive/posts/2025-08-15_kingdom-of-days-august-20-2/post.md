Yet another of the all-time great shows performed on this date–this time across the country at the Meadowlands, where Bruce and the band wrap up their ten-night Born in the U.S.A. stand. Steve Van Zandt makes his triumphant return to the E Street Band stage for the tour premiere of “” and the live debut of a powerful “.” The show is available as part of Bruce’s official archive series (get it ), where you can hear the strength of the bond between Bruce and Steve across the years. The Miami Horns also guest-star at this show, and the horn-fueled “” will be released in the box set.
