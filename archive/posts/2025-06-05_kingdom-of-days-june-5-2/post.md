Other highlights include the tour premiere of "[I'll Work for Your Love](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/02/14/roll-of-the-dice-ill-work-for-your-love/)" (acoustic), and a dedication of "[Tougher Than the Rest](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/08/08/roll-of-the-dice-tougher-than-the-rest/)" to the late, great Muhammad Ali.
