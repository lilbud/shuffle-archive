**1975:** Bruce works on “Thunder Road” in the studio at the Record Plant in New York City.

**1980:** Bruce finalizes the mix for “Ramrod” at Clover Studios in Los Angeles.

**1981:** One night after debuting “Can’t Help Falling in Love,” Bruce opens his second show in Paris by premiering his cover/adaptation of “Follow That Dream.” The encores include another world premiere: Bruce’s first cover of “Sweet Soul Music.”

**2014:** Record Store Day is a busy one: Bruce releases a new EP, *American Beauty*, performs the title track at soundcheck in Charlotte, and films it for release a few days later. That night, Bruce stuns the audience by opening with only the second-ever performance of “Iceman” and a cover-filled set that included “Louie Louie,” “Mustang Sally,” and “Brown-Eyed Girl.”
