***New for 2025:** New entry for 1977*<br>

**1971:** The Bruce Springsteen Band is in residence at the Student Prince in Asbury Park.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/11/1971-11-27.png.webp)

**1977:** Bruce holds a recording session at The Record Plant in New York City, where he and the band work on "[Racing in the Street](../2021-10-03_roll-of-the-dice-racing-in-the-street/post.md)."<br>

**1978:** Bruce and the E Street Band bring the Darkness Tour to the Dane County Memorial Coliseum in Madison, Wisconsin.<br>

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen, "Streets of Fire" - Madison, 1978-11-28](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=18_SB3a9Jbo)

**![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2019/11/47544237051_e03acb5d43_o.jpg.webp)**<br>

**1980:** On Black Friday, Bruce and the band play their second of two nights at Madison Square Garden in New York City. This show features a very unusual "[Ramrod](../2022-09-10_roll-of-the-dice-ramrod/post.md)"/"[Living on the Edge of the World](../2018-10-18_roll-of-the-dice-living-on-the-edge-of-the-world/post.md)" (the latter of which hadn't even been released) mashup, as well as the tour premiere of "[4th of July, Asbury Park (Sandy)](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/04/26/roll-of-the-dice-4th-of-july-asbury-park-sandy/)."<br>

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - "Ramrod/Living on the Edge of the World" - NYC, 1980-11-28](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4HUThNRDG0E)

[Watch on Youtube: Madison Square Garden New York 28/11/1980](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLJTyL9twk3zA4JUhmcl5oNlOk1Mn6t5dg)

**1999:** The first leg of the Reunion Tour enters its last stand when Bruce and the E Street Band play their first of two nights at the Target Center in Minneapolis. The holidays must be approaching–Bruce closes the show with the tour premiere of "[Santa Claus is Comin' to Town](../2021-12-24_cover-me-santa-claus-is-comin-to-town/post.md)."<br>

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - "Santa Claus is Comin' to Town" - Minneapolis, 1999-11-28](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WGTS8ii7nrU)

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/11/1999-11-28.png.webp)

**2007:** Bruce and the band play a fantastic Magic show in Milan to an Italian crowd that's on fire. For more highlights from this show, see my [*Where the Band Was* report.](../2018-11-28_where-the-band-was-milan-november-28-2007/post.md)<br>

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - Reason to Believe (Milan 28-11-07)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=piMODx-_KYk)

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - Last To Die (Milan)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s_-qAkqj8D8)

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - Milan 28-11-2007 - I'll Work For Your Love](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6RqWMY69A4I)

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - Tenth Avenue Freeze-Out (28-11-2007)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D5hgaIMDNpI)

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen Milan 28/11/2007 Full Concert](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZakunwBioKo)

**2008:** Bruce releases "[My Lucky Day,](../2019-09-02_roll-of-the-dice-my-lucky-day/post.md)" the second single from his upcoming album, *Working on a Dream*.<br>

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - My Lucky Day (Official Video)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gO7vFDfhDHw)

**2012:** Bruce continues shaking up the Wrecking Ball set list at the Rose Garden in Portland, opening with "[Land of Hope and Dreams](../2018-01-05_roll-of-the-dice-land-of-hope-and-dreams/post.md)," and featuring rare outings for "[Loose Ends](../2022-06-23_roll-of-the-dice-loose-end/post.md)," "[Seeds](../2020-10-10_roll-of-the-dice-seeds/post.md)," and "[Drive All Night](../2018-02-27_roll-of-the-dice-drive-all-night/post.md)." For more highlights from this show, see my [*Where the Band Was* report.](../2018-11-28_where-the-band-was-portland-november-28-2012/post.md)<br>

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen & the E Street Band - Loose Ends 2012-11-28 Live @ Rose Garden Arena, Portland, OR](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Zq_7TStsvg0)

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - Seeds (Live in Portland 2012-11-28)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=k8ZlKq7V9Sg)

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen & the E Street Band - Dancing in the Dark 2012-11-28 Live @ Rose Garden, Portland](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xo6m2n2Gr0s)

**2016:** Bruce resumes his promotional book tour, making an appearance at Books-a-Million in Chicago.<br>

[Watch on Youtube: Rocker Bruce Springsteen Stops In Chicago As Part Of Book Tour](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tyxMb8BCcUY)

[Watch on Youtube: Books-A-Million Chicago 11/28/16](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uKGsQY00vnc)

**2018:** *Springsteen on Broadway* begins the final leg of its theatrical run (minus Patti, who is absent tonight). In attendance: Patterson Hood of the Drive-By Truckers.<br>
