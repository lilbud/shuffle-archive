***New for 2025:** New entry for 1977*

**1974:** A scheduled E Street Band gig in Memphis is cancelled.

**2005:** In today’s installment of *Legends of Springsteen*, Bruce plays six songs to airport staff in the middle of the night during a brief connecting layover in Iceland on the way home from his European solo acoustic tour. This is Bruce’s only “performance” in Iceland to date.

**2013:** Bruce headlines Hard Rock Calling in London’s Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park, where Bruce plays his *Born in the U.S.A.* album start-to-finish. Zac Brown guests on “[Tenth Avenue Freeze-Out](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/11/19/roll-of-the-dice-tenth-avenue-freeze-out/),” and Bruce closes with an acoustic “[My Lucky Day](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/09/02/roll-of-the-dice-my-lucky-day/)” (replacing the setlisted “Thunder Road”). The full-album portion of the show will be included with Bruce’s *High Hopes* album the following year.
