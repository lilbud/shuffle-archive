At the Grammy Awards in 2003, Bruce paid tribute to the recently departed Joe Strummer by teaming up with Steve Van Zandt, Elvis Costello and Dave Grohl to cover The Clash’s classic “London Calling.”

[Watch on Youtube: The Clash Tribute - London Calling - Live](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MkyeGVuc2pQ)

[Watch on Youtube: The Clash - London Calling (Official Video)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EfK-WX2pa8c)

Bonus footage: Bruce and the E Street Band cover “London Calling” in concert at Hyde Park in 2009.

[Watch on Youtube: "London Calling" at the Hard Rock Calling Festival](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9o_YqNN4cDQ)

**First performed:** February 23, 2003 (New York City, NY)<br>
**Last performed:** October 29, 2009 (New York City, NY)<br>
