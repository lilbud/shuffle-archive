***New for 2025:** New entry for 1977*<br>

**1967:** The Castiles play a show at Le Teendezvous in New Shrewsbury, New Jersey.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/12/1967-1227.jpg.webp)

**1968:** Earth plays four sets at Le Teendezvous in New Shrewsbury.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/12/1968-1227.jpg.webp)

**1973:** Bruce and the E Street Band kick off a four-night/two-shows-nightly stand at The Main Point in Bryn Mawr, Pennsylvania.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/12/1973-1227.jpg.webp)

**1975:** Bruce and the band play their first of a four-night year-closing stand at the Tower Theater in Philadelphia. Tonight sees the tour premiere of "[Back in the U.S.A.](../1976-01-03_cover-me-back-in-the-u-s-a/post.md/)" and the last full-band "[Lost in the Flood](../2011-01-05_roll-of-the-dice-lost-in-the-flood/post.md/)" for 25 years.<br>

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - "Back in the USA" - Philadelphia, 1975-12-27](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MPcdBExC2sA)

[Watch on Youtube: Upper Darby 27/12/1975](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLJTyL9twk3zA1LKH8_99vA483OppBfqkI)

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/11/1975-12-27.png.webp)

**1977:** Bruce and the E Street Band hold a recording session for *Darkness on the Edge of Town* at The Record Plant in New York City.<br>

**1978:** The Darkness Tour heads into its last week with the first of two nights at the Stanley Theater in Pittsburgh.<br>

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - "Point Blank" - Pittsburgh, 1978-12-27](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KFstN5Spd_g)

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen, PM Magazine 1978](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NuJ-g62ekzc)

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2019/12/33668147698_f6fa581767_o.jpg.webp)

**1992:** Bruce joins Southside Johnny and The Asbury Jukes on stage at The Stone Pony for "[The Fever](../2020-10-04_roll-of-the-dice-the-fever/post.md)" and (with Jon Bon Jovi as well) "It's Been a Long Time" and "[Having a Party](../2021-12-31_matr-bruce-springsteen-and-southside-johnny-having-a-party/post.md)."<br>

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen & Southside Johnny - "The Fever" - Asbury Park, 1992-12-27](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ftWhs8awKcU)

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen, Southside Johnny, Jon Bon Jovi - Asbury Park, 1992-12-27](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EU1YWRzDSpo)

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/12/1992-12-27.png.webp)

**2017:** Patti Scialfa is sick with the flu, so Bruce continues playing the "B" setlist at *Springsteen on Broadway* at the Walter Kerr Theater in New York City.<br>
