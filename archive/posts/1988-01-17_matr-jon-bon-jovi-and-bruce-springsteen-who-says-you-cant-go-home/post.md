I’m not a big Bon Jovi fan, but I do love their song, “Who Says You Can’t Go Home,” from their 2005 *Have a Nice Day* album. That’s probably because it sounds just like a Bruce Springsteen song.

Seriously, just listen to the lyrics and tell me that Jon wasn’t channeling Bruce when he wrote it:

[https://www.youtube.com/embed/abzbVFuxigg?version=3&#038;rel=1&#038;showsearch=0&#038;showinfo=1&#038;iv_load_policy=1&#038;fs=1&#038;hl=en-US&#038;autohide=2&#038;wmode=transparent](https://www.youtube.com/embed/abzbVFuxigg?version=3&#038;rel=1&#038;showsearch=0&#038;showinfo=1&#038;iv_load_policy=1&#038;fs=1&#038;hl=en-US&#038;autohide=2&#038;wmode=transparent)

So I was pretty psyched when Jon asked Bruce to join him when he played “Who Says You Can’t Go Home” at the *12-12-12* benefit concert for the victims of Hurricane Sandy.

Sure enough, the song fit Bruce like a glove. Watch and see–it’s a fantastic performance:

[https://www.youtube.com/embed/NQWFklgKA_E?version=3&#038;rel=1&#038;showsearch=0&#038;showinfo=1&#038;iv_load_policy=1&#038;fs=1&#038;hl=en-US&#038;autohide=2&#038;wmode=transparent](https://www.youtube.com/embed/NQWFklgKA_E?version=3&#038;rel=1&#038;showsearch=0&#038;showinfo=1&#038;iv_load_policy=1&#038;fs=1&#038;hl=en-US&#038;autohide=2&#038;wmode=transparent)

As it turns out, “Who Says You Can’t Go Home” was made for a duet. In fact, Jon Bon Jovi recorded and filmed *two* versions of it for the same album. You’ve seen the first at the top of this post; here’s the second, in a country arrangement with Jennifer Nettles:

[https://www.youtube.com/embed/5CeX5VEo10c?version=3&#038;rel=1&#038;showsearch=0&#038;showinfo=1&#038;iv_load_policy=1&#038;fs=1&#038;hl=en-US&#038;autohide=2&#038;wmode=transparent](https://www.youtube.com/embed/5CeX5VEo10c?version=3&#038;rel=1&#038;showsearch=0&#038;showinfo=1&#038;iv_load_policy=1&#038;fs=1&#038;hl=en-US&#038;autohide=2&#038;wmode=transparent)

I gotta say: Bruce turned in a great performance, but the best “Who Says You Can’t Go Home” duet award has to go to Jon and Jennifer.
