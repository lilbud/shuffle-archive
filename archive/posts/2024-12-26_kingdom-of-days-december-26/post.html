New for 2024: No updates

1975: Bruce drops by the Bottom Line in New York City to catch Patti Smith’s late show, providing backing vocals for “Gloria.”

1995: Bruce announces an extension of his solo acoustic tour in the new year, with select dates in North America before heading overseas.

2017: Patti Scialfa is sick with the flu and cannot perform, throwing a monkey wrench into the Springsteen on Broadway storyline and set list. Bruce adapts by changing that part of the show to focus on his kids instead of his wife, and accordingly, “Long Time Comin’” makes its first appearance in the show, in the spot where “Tougher Than the Rest” would normally be. Bruce also swaps out “Brilliant Disguise” for “The Ghost of Tom Joad,” giving added weight to the segment on his social values.
