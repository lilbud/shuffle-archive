I try to keep a rotating spotlight on fresh new cover artists here, but every once in a while an artist earns a second look.

Today makes the first time I've featured a three-peat, however. That's because Los Angeles-based funk combo Scary Pockets is just *so* darn good, and it seems like every year at this time they gift us with a creative take on one of Bruce Springsteen's classic songs.

First, there was their [incredibly cool cover](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/12/01/cover-me-scary-pockets-and-darren-criss-dancing-in-the-dark/) of "[Dancing in the Dark](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2021/12/25/roll-of-the-dice-dancing-in-the-dark/)," featuring Darrin Criss on lead vocals back in 2019. Then there was the time Rett Madison fronted [an infectiously danceable version](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/11/10/cover-me-scary-pockets-im-on-fire/) of "[I'm on Fire](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2021/09/11/roll-of-the-dice-im-on-fire/)" last year.

Today, Swatkins and Therese Curatolo front the band for a funky cover of a coverâ€“Manfred Mann's version of Bruce's "[Blinded by the Light](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2023/01/01/roll-of-the-dice-blinded-by-the-light/)."

[Watch on Youtube: Blinded by the Light | Bruce Springsteen | funk cover ft. Swatkins & Therese Curatolo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QZMP_HrAwCg)

The group tips their hat to Bruce for the original, but it's clear they take their inspiration from Manfred Mann's adaptation, which went to #1 on the Billboard Hot 100 in 1977. And it works perfectly, quickly settling into a irresistible groove.

Scary Pockets is headed out on tour across the U.S. and Canada next month, and with at least three Springsteen songs in their repertoire you might hear a familiar song or two if you catch them in person. Check out their tour schedule [here](https://www.scarypocketsfunk.com/).
