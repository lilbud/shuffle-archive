**1980:** Bruce works on mixes for his upcoming album *The River* at Clover Studios in Los Angeles.

**1981:** A scheduled River Tour show in Paris for this date was moved to April 18.

**1983:** Bruce works on “Cynthia” at his home studio in Los Angeles.
