***New for 2025:** New entry for 1972 ,1978, 2018, and 2024; audio for 1974*<br>

**1967:** The Castiles play the Hullabaloo in Middletown, New Jersey.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/02/1967-0310.jpg.webp)

**1968:** The destined-for-legend Upstage club prepares to open for business in Asbury Park, and the local paper publishes a profile.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/03/1968-03-10.jpg.webp)

**1972:** The Bruce Springsteen Band performs at St. Joseph High School mixer in Metuchen, New Jersey. (The school mangles his name a bit, though.)<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2025/03/medium-2.jpg.webp)

**1974:** Bruce and the still unnamed E Street Band play two shows at Houston's Liberty Hall. The late show features the concert debut of "[The Fever](../2020-10-04_roll-of-the-dice-the-fever/post.md)," "[Gimme That Wine](../2019-03-22_cover-me-gimme-that-wine/post.md)" and "[Angel's Blues](../2019-10-05_roll-of-the-dice-angels-blues/post.md)."<br>

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - THE FEVER 1974 (audio)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=efLelUQkir0)

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - SHES' SO FINE 1974 (audio)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=q3NE0JgRRC8)

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - Angel's Blues - Live at Liberty Hall, Houston (03/10/1974)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DlpF_bOvKkg)

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - 1974-03-10 Liberty Hall, Houston, TX](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hwbiB8wJAJA)

**1977:** An early version of "[Don't Look Back](../2020-05-03_roll-of-the-dice-dont-look-back/post.md)" makes its debut at the Sports Arena in Toledo.<br>

[Watch Video Highlight](https://videopress.com/embed/ofg8Vn4K)

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2021/03/1977-0310.jpg.webp)

**1978:** Bruce and the band complete their recording of "[Darkness on the Edge of Town](../2016-01-07_roll-of-the-dice-darkness-on-the-edge-of-town/post.md)" at the Record Plant in New York City.<br>

[Watch on Youtube: Darkness On the Edge of Town](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0LpdEyGhNxM)

**1983:** Bruce records "[The Klansman](../1983-01-16_roll-of-the-dice-the-klansman/post.md)" in his home studio. This haunting gem remains unreleased to this day.<br>

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen-The Klansman](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1ewhMge_MXM)

**1989:** At Mickey Rourke's Rubber Club in Los Angeles, Bruce joins The Mighty Hornets for "[C.C. Rider](../2021-08-29_cover-me-c-c-rider/post.md)."<br>

**1999:** Bruce and the E Street Band rehearse for their upcoming Reunion Tour at Asbury Park's Convention Hall. News coverage below.<br>

[Watch on Youtube: News coverage of E Street Band Rehearsals for the Reunion Tour](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iWe1tQGBS9g)

**2000:** Bruce and the reunited E Street Band close out the Florida leg of their Reunion Tour with a second show in Sunrise.<br>
