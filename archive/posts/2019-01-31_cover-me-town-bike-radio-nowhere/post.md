C’mon, admit it: who among us hasn’t wondered what it would sound like if The Ramones and The Go-Gos teamed up to cover “[Radio Nowhere](../2021-08-01_roll-of-the-dice-radio-nowhere/post.md)?”

Well, wonder no longer. because this delightful cover by Liverpool band Town Bike is pretty much exactly that. And I love it so much!

[Watch on Youtube: Radio Nowhere (Springsteen cover) - Town bike](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lEZj_4tlDSA)

Kudos to Town Bike for realizing that “Radio Nowhere” was always a punk rocker at heart.
