From their 2008 EP by the same name, here’s a lovely acoustic cover of “[Brilliant Disguise](../1987-01-09_roll-of-the-dice-brilliant-disguise/post.md/)” by Canadian band, The Reason. Most of the song is brash and bright, but the band grows quieter as the song becomes more contemplative, to great effect. A very nice cover of one of my all-time favorites.
