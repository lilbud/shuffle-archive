Everyone knows Manfred Mann’s cover of Bruce’s “[Blinded by the Light](../2011-01-02_roll-of-the-dice-blinded-by-the-light/post.md).” True Springsteen fans know Manfred Mann’s Earth Band [also covered](../2018-06-14_cover-me-manfred-manns-earth-band-for-you/post.md) “[For You](../2011-01-07_roll-of-the-dice-for-you/post.md)” and “[Spirit in the Night](../2011-01-08_roll-of-the-dice-spirit-in-the-night/post.md/).”

But the band’s most obscure Springsteen adaptation is actually a cover of Bruce’s greatest hit.

From 2009, check out this brilliant mash-up of “[Dancing in the Dark](../1984-01-13_roll-of-the-dice-dancing-in-the-dark/post.md)” with “House of the Rising Sun” by The Animals.
