Everyone knows Manfred Mann’s cover of Bruce’s “Blinded by the Light.” True Springsteen fans know Manfred Mann’s Earth Band also covered “For You” and “Spirit in the Night.”

But the band’s most obscure Springsteen adaptation is actually a cover of Bruce’s greatest hit.

From 2009, check out this brilliant mash-up of “Dancing in the Dark” with “House of the Rising Sun” by The Animals.
