New for 2025:  No updates

1965: Castiles drummer Bart Haynes quits the band. A year later, Bart will join the Marines and ship out to Vietnam, where he will be killed in action. Bruce will remember Bart often–in his shows, in print, and on Broadway.

Watch on Youtube: Born In the U.S.A. (Introduction Part 2) (Springsteen on Broadway - Official Audio)

1971: The Bruce Springsteen Band opens for Cactus at Virginia Commonwealth University in Richmond.

Watch on Youtube: BRUCE SPRINGSTEEN & THE STEEL MILL BAND 1971

1973: Bruce and the boys headline two shows at The Main Point in Bryn Mawr, Pennsylvania–their second of a three-night stand. Richard Blackwell makes a guest appearance on congas.

1976: Patti Smith guest-stars on Night Three (of six) at the Palladium in New York City, joining Bruce and the Band for an unusual version of “Rosalita (Come Out Tonight).”

Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen (w/ Patti Smith) - ROSALITA - LAND OF A 1000 DANCES (1976)

1980: Bruce and the E Street Band kick off a four-night stand at the Los Angeles Sports Arena, where Flo and Eddie contribute guest backing vocals to “Hungry Heart.”

Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen (feat. Flo & Eddie), "Hungry Heart" Los Angeles, 1980-10-30

1984: Bruce releases the latest single, the title track from his mega-hit Born in the U.S.A. album.

Watch on Youtube: Born in the U.S.A.

1987: Bruce premieres the video for his latest single, “Brilliant Disguise,” the first single from Tunnel of Love. Watch the video and news coverage below.

Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - Brilliant Disguise (Official Video)

Watch on Youtube: Entertainment Tonight - 1987, Bruce Springsteen's "Brilliant Disguise"

Also today, Bruce donates a complete signed collection of his albums to The Rainbow Foundation to raise money via auction for local children in need.

1992: Bruce and his new touring band bring World Tour 1992 to Iowa State University in Ames.

Watch Video Highlight

2005: Bruce’s solo acoustic show at the TD Banknorth Garden in Boston is filmed but will remain unreleased with one exception: a video for “Matamoros Banks” that Bruce will release fourteen years later in support of Project Adelante. In the encores, Bruce tour premieres “Dirty Water” as a Boston special. In attendance tonight: Senator John Kerry.

Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen Boston M.A. 10-30-05

2006: Bruce and the Sessions Band complete their Scandinavian leg with a show at the Globe Arena in Stockholm, where Bruce tour premieres “Jesus Was an Only Son.”

Watch on Youtube: Stockholm 30/10/2006

Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen, The River, Seeger Sessions, Stockholm 30/10/06

2007: At Night Two in Los Angeles on the Magic Tour, Bruce celebrates Halloween a day early, once again (as in Halloweens past) emerging from a coffin to start the show. Bruce also tour premieres “Kitty’s Back” during the encores.

2009: Bruce and Roy make a guest appearance with U2 and Patti Smith at Night Two of the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame 25th Anniversary Concerts.

Watch on Youtube: U2, Bruce Springsteen and Patti Smith perform "Because the Night" 25th Anniversary shows

Watch on Youtube: U2 & Bruce Springsteen - "I Still Haven't Found What I'm Looking For"

2012: A scheduled Wrecking Ball show in Rochester is postponed to the following day due to Hurricane Sandy.

2014: Bruce is interviewed for the New York Times book section, where he provides his reading recommendations.

2018: Springsteen on Broadway continues its theatrical run at the Walter Kerr Theater in New York City. In attendance tonight: Jimmy Fallon and former (and soon-to-be) Speaker of the House Nancy Pelosi.
