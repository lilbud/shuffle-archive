***New for 2025:** New entry for 1977 and 2024; video for 2023*

**1949:** “Professor” Roy Bittan is born in Rockaway Beach, New York.

![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/roy-300x300.jpeg)

**1966:** The Castiles play an opening set for The Rogues at the new Surf ‘n’ See Club in Sea Bright, New Jersey.

**1967:** The “Fabulous” Castiles play a gig at the See ‘n’ Surf Club (not a typo–same club, new name) to mark its first anniversary.

![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/1967-0702.jpg)

**1971:** The Sundance Blues Band plays the Upstage in Asbury Park.

![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/07/1971-07-02-300x102.jpg)

**1976:** Mike Appel sends Bruce a letter informing him that he is prohibited from recording new music due to their ongoing legal dispute. Bruce will spend 1976 and much of 1977 touring instead of recording as a result.

**1977:** Bruce and the E Street Band record “[Don’t Look Back](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/05/03/roll-of-the-dice-dont-look-back/)” at Atlantic Studios in New York City, and Bruce works on a beautiful early solo piano arrangement of “[Racing in the Street](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2021/10/03/roll-of-the-dice-racing-in-the-street/).”

[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pvMn2zFVX0E](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pvMn2zFVX0E)

**1981:** Bruce and the E Street Band kick off the final leg of the River Tour by playing the first-ever event (by anyone) at the Meadowlands. Bruce debuts his cover of “[Jersey Girl](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/07/24/cover-me-roll-of-the-dice-jersey-girl/)” for the occasion; other highlights include the tour premiere of “[I Don’t Want to Go Home](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/03/30/meeting-across-the-river-bruce-springsteen-and-steve-van-zandt-i-dont-want-to-go-home/)” and the last-ever “Born to Run” opener.

[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wXYdQPwPyF8](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wXYdQPwPyF8)

[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LGKg3gEMCUU](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LGKg3gEMCUU)

[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5eKi-BMbfRY](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5eKi-BMbfRY)

![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/1981-07-02.jpg)

**1984:** The last of three nights in St. Paul (and the opening stand of the Born in the U.S.A. Tour) features the live debuts of “[Downbound Train](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/01/22/roll-of-the-dice-downbound-train/)” and “[Cover Me](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2021/01/13/roll-of-the-dice-cover-me/),” along with the last full-band arrangement of “[No Surrender](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2021/11/18/roll-of-the-dice-no-surrender/)” for fifteen years.

[https://videopress.com/embed/PWy5WzaZ](https://videopress.com/embed/PWy5WzaZ)

**1986:** On the CBS television network, *West 57th* airs a segment profiling Clarence.

[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3QvN4MwV3wk](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3QvN4MwV3wk)

**1988:** The Tunnel of Love Tour comes to Stockholm for the first of two nights. The second night will make headlines, but Night One still provides a great show, featuring the tour premiere of “[Cadillac Ranch](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/07/23/roll-of-the-dice-cadillac-ranch/).”

[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wBU8DiGV6j0](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wBU8DiGV6j0)

**1989:** Bruce joins Gary U.S. Bonds at The Cafe Bar in Long Branch for six songs: “[This Little Girl,](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/09/06/rotd-matr-cover-me-gary-u-s-bonds-bruce-springsteen-the-e-street-band-this-little-girl/)” “[Angelyne](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/02/04/cover-me-gary-u-s-bonds-and-the-e-street-band-angelyne/),” “[Bony Moronie](https://youtu.be/3QvN4MwV3wk),” “[Your Love](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/07/27/cover-me-gary-u-s-bonds-your-love/),” “[Dedication,](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/06/17/cover-me-roll-of-the-dice-gary-u-s-bonds-dedication/)” and “[New Orleans](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/07/31/cover-me-new-orleans/).”

**1992:** A scheduled show in Barcelona is postponed by two days due to a transport worker strike.

**1999:** Bruce films a cameo performance in the movie *High Fidelity* at Disney Soundstage Studios in New York City.
