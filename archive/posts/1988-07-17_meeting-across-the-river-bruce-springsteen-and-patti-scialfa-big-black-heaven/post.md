Patti gets the spotlight today From her 1993 debut album *Rumble Doll*, here's "Big Black Heaven," accompanied by Bruce on guitar and keyboards.

[Watch on Youtube: Watch Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Um1jjOREkkg)

Like much of the album it comes from, "Big Black Heaven" draws heavily from Bruce's and Patti's relationship, mirroring the vulnerability and emotional bravery laced through Bruce's own writing from this period.

**Big Black Heaven**<br>
**Recorded:** March 1993<br>
**Released:** *Rumble Doll* (1993)<br>
**Never performed**<br>
