Oklahoma singer-songwriter Jimmy LaFave has a talent for interpreting Bruce’s songs. (See his cover of “Valentine’s Day”.)

In 2012, Jimmy released his album *Depending on the Distance*, featuring his cover of Bruce’s “Land of Hope and Dreams.” Unlike Bruce’s version, which plays more like a declarative anthem, Jimmy’s version is a tender and intimate love song. It’s a different take, and a great cover.
