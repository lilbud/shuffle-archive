Bruce Springsteen’s Western Stars album gets a lot of deserved attention for its lush arrangements, and the film version only ups the ante with a full-on orchestra.

But as much as I love those arrangements, sometimes all it takes is a simple piano to make you fully appreciate just how gorgeous Bruce’s melodies can be.

Case in point: Marcel Lichter’s solo piano instrumental rendition of the album’s title track–perfect for a quiet weekend morning.

Enjoy.
