New for 2024: Audio for 1975

1967: The Castiles play a show at Le Teendezvous in New Shrewsbury, New Jersey.

1968: Earth plays four sets at Le Teendezvous in New Shrewsbury.

1973: Bruce and the E Street Band kick off a four-night/two-shows-nightly stand at The Main Point in Bryn Mawr, Pennsylvania.

1975: Bruce and the band play their first of a four-night year-closing stand at the Tower Theater in Philadelphia. Tonight sees the tour premiere of “Back in the U.S.A.” and the last full-band “Lost in the Flood” for 25 years.

1978: The Darkness Tour heads into its last week with the first of two nights at the Stanley Theater in Pittsburgh.

1992: Bruce joins Southside Johnny and The Asbury Jukes on stage at The Stone Pony for “The Fever” and (with Jon Bon Jovi as well) “It’s Been a Long Time” and “Having a Party.”

2017: Patti Scialfa is sick with the flu, so Bruce continues playing the “B” setlist at Springsteen on Broadway at the Walter Kerr Theater in New York City.
