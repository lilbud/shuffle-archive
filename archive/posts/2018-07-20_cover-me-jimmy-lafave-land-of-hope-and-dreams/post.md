Oklahoma singer-songwriter Jimmy LaFave has a talent for interpreting Bruce's songs (see his cover of "[Valentine's Day](../2018-03-03_cover-me-jimmy-lafave-valentines-day/post.md)").

In 2012, Jimmy released his album *Depending on the Distance*, featuring his cover of Bruce's "[Land of Hope and Dreams.](../2018-01-05_roll-of-the-dice-land-of-hope-and-dreams/post.md)" Unlike Bruce's version, which plays more like a declarative anthem, Jimmy's version is tender–almost a love song. It's a different take, and a great cover.
