**1975:** <br>
Bruce drops by the Bottom Line in New York City to catch Patti Smith's late show, providing backing vocals for "[Gloria](../2018-03-16_cover-me-gloria/post.md)."

**1995:**<br>

Bruce announces an extension of his solo acoustic tour in the new year, with select dates in North America before heading overseas.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/main/assets_webp/2021/12/1995-12-2t6.jpg.webp)

**2017:**<br>
Patti Scialfa is sick with the flu and cannot perform, throwing a monkey wrench into the *Springsteen on Broadway* storyline and set list. Bruce adapts by changing that part of the show to focus on his kids instead of his wife, and accordingly, "[Long Time Comin'](../2022-06-19_roll-of-the-dice-long-time-comin/post.md)" makes its first appearance in the show, in the spot where "[Tougher Than the Rest](../1987-01-02_roll-of-the-dice-tougher-than-the-rest/post.md/)" would normally be. Bruce also swaps out "[Brilliant Disguise](../1987-01-09_roll-of-the-dice-brilliant-disguise/post.md/)" for "[The Ghost of Tom Joad](../2019-10-17_roll-of-the-dice-the-ghost-of-tom-joad/post.md)," giving added weight to the segment on his social values.
