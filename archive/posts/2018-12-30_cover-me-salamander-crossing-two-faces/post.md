I'm a sucker for obscure Bruce covers and unusual arrangements; Salamander Crossing checked both boxes with their bluegrass rendition of "[Two Faces](../2022-05-26_roll-of-the-dice-two-faces/post.md)" on their 1996 album *Passion Train.*

The band isn't around anymore, but their career retrospective album (which includes "Two Faces") is still in print.
