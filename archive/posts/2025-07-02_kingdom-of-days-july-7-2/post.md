**1977:** [Don’t Look Back](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/05/03/roll-of-the-dice-dont-look-back/)

Bruce and the band play one of their most legendary shows when the Darkness Tour takes over the Roxy Theater in West Hollywood. The show is a surprise, announced only the day prior, and tickets are hard to come by, as the venue only holds 500 people. Bruce opens with his debut of “” and later covers “” for the first time as well. More significantly, this show also sees the premiere of an early version of “” in a very different form, along with the debut of “.” The show is broadcast live on KMET-FM, and Bruce’s performance of “” will eventually serve as the B-side for his single for a live performance of “,” released in 1987.
