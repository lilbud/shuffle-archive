*Asbury Park Press*



![](https://i0.wp.com/estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Asbury_Park_Press_Tue__May_11__1993_-scaled.jpg?resize=758%2C712&amp;ssl=1)

One of the standout shows of the first leg of the Devils & Dust Tour. At the Rosemont Theater outside of Chicago, Bruce debuts “Dream Baby Dream” (with slightly different lyrics and phrasing than the version it would soon morph into) and plays the banjo for the first time (on “I’m on Fire”). During “Land of Hope and Dreams,” Bruce breaks a guitar string and continues a capella without missing a beat until Kevin Buell restrings the guitar in the nick of time–a truly remarkable performance.
