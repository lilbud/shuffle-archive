Mark Lavengood is no stranger to covering Bruce Springsteen–his bluegrass cover of “Hungry Heart” from three years ago is well worth seeking out.

But his new dobro-centric cover of “Born in the U.S.A.” from his recent tour is even better, and luckily for us, he’s released an official tour compilation video that uses that song as its soundtrack.

Take a look and listen to Lavengood’s terrific adaptation of one of Bruce’s most iconic songs.
