1973: Bruce continues his five-night stand at The Quiet Knight in Chicago, opening for The Persuasions.  Earlier in the day, The New York Daily News reviews his first album.

1974: Bruce and the E Street Band headline at The Mosque in Richmond, Virginia.

1990: At Soundworks West in Los Angeles, Bruce records “Sad Eyes” with Randy Jackson, David Sancious, Jeff Porcaro, and Michael Fisher. It’ll stay in the vault for eight years before debuting on Tracks.

Watch on Youtube: Sad Eyes (Studio Outtake - 1990)

1996: Bruce plays the Music Hall in Austin, Texas, with Joe Ely joining for a final encore of “Blowin’ Down This Road.” After the show, Bruce and Joe catch Willie Nelson’s show nearby.

2009: The video for “The Wrestler” is filmed at the New Brunswick Boxing Gym.

2014: Bruce and the E Street Band continue rehearsing in Cape Town for their upcoming High Hopes tour.

2017: Night Two of three in Perth brings a shift in the show structure as Bruce abandons the chronological set list that characterized the end of the 2016 tour and first night of the 2017 tour, reverting to a more familiar, casual fan-friendly set list.

2018: Springsteen on Broadway continues its theatrical run at the Walter Kerr Theater in New York City.

2019: Bruce and Peter Wolf drop by Mr. Music in Boston and spend about 40 minutes in the music store hanging out and browsing guitars.

2023: Bruce and the E Street Band rehearse for their upcoming tour at Cure Insurance Arena in Trenton.
