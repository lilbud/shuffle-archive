***New for 2025:** Review for 1988 and 1996*

**1973:** Bruce and the band open for Blood, Sweat and Tears in Santa Monica.

![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/1973-03-03.jpg)

![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/1973-0303-300x263.jpg)

**1974:** Bruce and the band play two shows at Georgetown University in Washington, DC. The first show’s set list features the entire *Wild & Innocent* album, the last known such occurrence for 35 years. The late show is broadcast on campus radio and features the last performance of “[4th of July, Asbury Park (Sandy)](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/04/26/roll-of-the-dice-4th-of-july-asbury-park-sandy/)” with its original lyrics until 2005.
