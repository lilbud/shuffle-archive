New for 2025: Review for 1988 and 1996

1973: Bruce and the band open for Blood, Sweat and Tears in Santa Monica.

1974: Bruce and the band play two shows at Georgetown University in Washington, DC. The first show’s set list features the entire Wild & Innocent album, the last known such occurrence for 35 years. The late show is broadcast on campus radio and features the last performance of “4th of July, Asbury Park (Sandy)” with its original lyrics until 2005.
