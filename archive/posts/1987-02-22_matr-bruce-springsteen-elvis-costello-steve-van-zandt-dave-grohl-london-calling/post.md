At the Grammy Awards in 2003, Bruce paid tribute to the recently departed Joe Strummer by teaming up with Steve Van Zandt, Elvis Costello and Dave Grohl to cover The Clash’s classic “London Calling.”

[Watch on Youtube: Watch Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MkyeGVuc2pQ)

The original 1979 single and title track is arguably the band’s signature song. It’s also their most summational, capturing their sound, smarts, and eye on current events within a single track.

[Watch on Youtube: Watch Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EfK-WX2pa8c)

“London Calling” just barely missed the Top Ten in the UK, and it didn’t chart at all in America. But it grew in stature over the years, and in 2004 *Rolling Stone* ranked it #15 in their 500 Greatest Songs of All Time.

Bonus footage: Bruce and the E Street Band cover “London Calling” in concert at Hyde Park in 2009.
