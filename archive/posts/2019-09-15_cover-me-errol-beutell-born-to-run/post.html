Long time readers know that I have a rule when it comes to covering Bruce: either do it better or do it different–or don’t bother.

But there are exceptions to every rule, and one-man cover band Errol Beutell is our case in point today.

Errol’s version of “Born to Run” may hew closely–very closely–to the original  album arrangement, but he brings such passion, joy, prowess, resourcefulness, and chutzpah to his rendition of Bruce’s iconic anthem that I can’t help but break out in a big, goofy grin every time I watch it.
