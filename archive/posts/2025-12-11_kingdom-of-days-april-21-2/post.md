The Bruce Springsteen Band headlines at Red Bank Catholic High School in Red Bank, New Jersey.

**1972:**



![](https://i0.wp.com/estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/1972-04-21.jpg?resize=687%2C824&amp;ssl=1)



![](https://i0.wp.com/estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/1972-0421.jpg?resize=758%2C496&amp;ssl=1)

Bruce and the E Street Band play a sparsely attended show at Knoxville Civic Auditorium in Knoxville, Tennessee.

**1976:**

At Clover Studios in Los Angeles, Bruce works on the final mixes for “You Can Look (But You Better Not Touch)” and “Held Up Without a Gun.”

**1980:**

[https://www.youtube.com/embed/2QFJkYm-kYk?feature=oembed](https://www.youtube.com/embed/2QFJkYm-kYk?feature=oembed)

[https://www.youtube.com/embed/gxBhIeTARBE?feature=oembed](https://www.youtube.com/embed/gxBhIeTARBE?feature=oembed)

Bruce brings the River Tour to Spain (his first show in that country) for a show at the Palau Municipal D’Esports in Barcelona. Ten songs are filmed, and you can watch them below.

**1981:**

[https://www.youtube.com/embed/8UHJCg-BRNc?list=PL05AACA487AEB54F3](https://www.youtube.com/embed/8UHJCg-BRNc?list=PL05AACA487AEB54F3)

[https://www.youtube.com/embed/videoseries?list=PLJTyL9twk3zD1dum-kCInli_oIsWTc5zX](https://www.youtube.com/embed/videoseries?list=PLJTyL9twk3zD1dum-kCInli_oIsWTc5zX)

Also today: Gary U.S. Bonds releases his comeback album, produced by Stevie and Bruce and featuring three original songs and vocal and instrumental performances by Bruce.

*Dedication,*

[https://www.youtube.com/embed/videoseries?list=PLp6WWcZCHeSjGcXK0zGNeNmaNxB4g5WKR](https://www.youtube.com/embed/videoseries?list=PLp6WWcZCHeSjGcXK0zGNeNmaNxB4g5WKR)

Bruce works on “Cynthia” at his home studio in Los Angeles.

**1983:**

[https://www.youtube.com/embed/SqpZGA_kEjE?feature=oembed](https://www.youtube.com/embed/SqpZGA_kEjE?feature=oembed)

Bruce guests with Clarence Clemons and the Red Bank Rockers at Expo in Mount Ivy, New York.

**1984:**

Bruce and the E Street Band bring the Reunion Tour to Charlotte for a single show at the Coliseum.

![](https://i0.wp.com/estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/2000-04-21b.jpg?resize=593%2C985&amp;ssl=1)

Bruce plays his first public warm-up show for his upcoming solo tour, a benefit for World Hunger Year at the Paramount in Asbury Park. Several tracks get their live premieres and “Part Man, Part Monkey” is played for the first time in 17 years.

**2005:** *Devils & Dust*

[https://www.youtube.com/embed/C7kHCfWJ6mY?feature=oembed](https://www.youtube.com/embed/C7kHCfWJ6mY?feature=oembed)

![](https://i0.wp.com/estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/2005-04-21.jpg?resize=758%2C338&amp;ssl=1)

Bruce attends the annual Kirsten Ann Carr Fund gala (along with Elvis Costello) at the Tribeca Grill in New York.

**2007:**



![](https://i0.wp.com/estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/04/elvis.jpg?resize=400%2C291&amp;ssl=1)

Bruce eulogizes Danny Federici at Danny’s funeral in Red Bank. The band’sscheduled show in Tampa is postponed to the following day.

**2008:**



![](https://i0.wp.com/estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/2008-04-21.jpg?resize=758%2C644&amp;ssl=1)

At the first of two nights at Boston’s TD Garden, Bruce and the band play “m” (by request) for the first time in 25 years.
