New for 2025: Video for 2023

1971: Bruce jams with local musicians at an open mic night at The Upstage in Asbury Park.

1977: Bruce continues his Florida swing with a gig at Jai Alai Fronton in Orlando.

1981: At Market Square Arena in Indianapolis, Bruce and the E Street Band wrap up the first leg of the River Tour and play “Fade Away” for the last time until 2005.

1999: Bruce and the reunited E Street Band rehearse for their upcoming Reunion Tour at Convention Hall in Asbury Park.

2001: “Cover Me” and “My Hometown” are both certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of America.

2009: Working on a Dream is certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of America.

2023: Bruce and the E Street Band bring their World Tour to Saint Paul, where they tour premiere “Pay Me My Money Down” and “Working on the Highway.”
