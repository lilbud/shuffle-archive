New for 2025: New entry for 1979 and 2024; audio for 2000; video for 2003

1973: Chicago cancels their concert in Buffalo at the last minute, scuttling Bruce’s opening act performance along with it.

1978: Another abrupt cancellation: Bruce’s scheduled Darkness Tour stop in La Crosse, Wisconsin is advertised right up until the day before the show before cancelling without public explanation.

1979: Bruce works on “Ramrod” during a recording session at The Power Station in New York City.

Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen Ramrod #1

1982: Bruce joins Jackson Browne on stage for “The Promised Land” and “Running on Empty” in New York’s Central Park at the Rally for Disarmament. Later that night, Bruce plays three songs with Sonny Kenn at Big Man’s West in Red Bank.

2005: A scheduled solo acoustic show in Berlin is rescheduled after Terry Magovern’s  fiancée passes away.

2007: Bruce joins Jackson Browne on stage in Red Bank for two encore performances at a benefit for People with ALS.

2011: Clarence Clemons suffers a severe stroke, and his friends and family rush to his side.
