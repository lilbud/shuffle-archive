**1979:** The last night of the Darkness Tour in Richfield is also the longest. The 31-song setlist is highlighted by fond farewells (for a time, at least): “[The Fever](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/10/04/roll-of-the-dice-the-fever/)” and “[Meeting Across the River](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/08/04/roll-of-the-dice-meeting-across-the-river/)” won’t appear again for two decades, and “[Streets of Fire](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2021/11/06/roll-of-the-dice-streets-of-fire/)” will be absent for 24 years.

[Watch on Youtube: Richfield 01/01/1979](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLJTyL9twk3zDRD_Yf7sSE5kXut3NQgaUK)

**1987:** Bruce releases “[Fire](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/08/05/roll-of-the-dice-fire/),” his latest single from the *Live 1975-85* box set collection.

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - Commercial for "Fire" - 1987-01-01](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=grhe4slilF8)
