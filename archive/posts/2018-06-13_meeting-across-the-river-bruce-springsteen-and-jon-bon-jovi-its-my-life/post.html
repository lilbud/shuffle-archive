A one-time-only performance: Jon Bon Jovi makes a guest appearance at Bruce’s 2003 holiday show in Asbury Park, and the two perform Bon Jovi’s “It’s My Life” together.

Bon Jovi’s original 2000 single peaked at #33 in the U.S., but it topped the charts in other countries and re-established the band for the new century.

It’s My Life
First performed: December 7, 2003 (Asbury Park, NJ)
Last performed: December 7, 2003 (Asbury Park, NJ)
