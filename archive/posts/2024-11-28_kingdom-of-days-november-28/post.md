***New for 2024:** Video for 2012*

**1971:** The Bruce Springsteen Band is in residence at the Student Prince in Asbury Park.

![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/11/1971-11-27.png)

**1978:** Bruce and the E Street Band bring the Darkness Tour to the Dane County Memorial Coliseum in Madison, Wisconsin.

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen, "Streets of Fire" - Madison, 1978-11-28](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=18_SB3a9Jbo)

**![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/47544237051_e03acb5d43_o.jpg)**

**1980:** On Black Friday, Bruce and the band play their second of two nights at Madison Square Garden in New York City. This show features a very unusual “[Ramrod](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2022/09/10/roll-of-the-dice-ramrod/)“/”[Living on the Edge of the World](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/10/18/roll-of-the-dice-living-on-the-edge-of-the-world/)” (the latter of which hadn’t even been released) mashup, as well as the tour premiere of “[4th of July, Asbury Park (Sandy)](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/04/26/roll-of-the-dice-4th-of-july-asbury-park-sandy/).”

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - "Ramrod/Living on the Edge of the World" - NYC, 1980-11-28](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4HUThNRDG0E)

[Watch on Youtube: Madison Square Garden New York 28/11/1980](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLJTyL9twk3zA4JUhmcl5oNlOk1Mn6t5dg)

**1999:** The first leg of the Reunion Tour enters its last stand when Bruce and the E Street Band play their first of two nights at the Target Center in Minneapolis. The holidays must be approaching–Bruce closes the show with the tour premiere of “[Santa Claus is Comin’ to Town](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2021/12/24/cover-me-santa-claus-is-comin-to-town/).”

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - "Santa Claus is Comin' to Town" - Minneapolis, 1999-11-28](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WGTS8ii7nrU)

![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/11/1999-11-28.png)

**2008:** Bruce releases “[My Lucky Day,](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/09/02/roll-of-the-dice-my-lucky-day/)” the second single from his upcoming album, *Working on a Dream*.

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - My Lucky Day (Video Version)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gO7vFDfhDHw)

**2012:** Bruce continues shaking up the Wrecking Ball set list at the Rose Garden in Portland, opening with “[Land of Hope and Dreams](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/01/05/roll-of-the-dice-land-of-hope-and-dreams/),” and featuring rare outings for “[Loose Ends](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2022/06/23/roll-of-the-dice-loose-end/),” “[Seeds](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/10/10/roll-of-the-dice-seeds/),” and “[Drive All Night](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/02/27/roll-of-the-dice-drive-all-night/).” For more highlights from this show, see my [*Where the Band Was* report.](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/11/28/where-the-band-was-portland-november-28-2012/)

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen & the E Street Band - Loose Ends 2012-11-28 Live @ Rose Garden Arena, Portland, OR](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Zq_7TStsvg0)
