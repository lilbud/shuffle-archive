***New for 2025:** No updates*<br>

**1974:** Bruce stops by KLOL-FM in Houston in the afternoon for an interview with DJ Ed Beauchamp.<br>

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen- Interview (The Lost Radio Show)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kzXpb_tv7-0)

That evening, Bruce and the (still unnamed) E Street Band play two shows at Liberty Hall.

**1975:** The first of two nights at DAR Constitution Hall in Washington, DC.<br>

[Watch Video Highlight](https://videopress.com/embed/4B0zuMOh)

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/main/assets_webp/2021/03/1975-0308.jpg.webp)

**1982:** In a Los Angeles recording studio, Bruce gives his song "[Protection](../2021-12-26_rotd-cover-me-donna-summer-protection/post.md)" to Donna Summer and provides backing vocals and guitar on the track, while Roy plays piano.<br>

[Watch on Youtube: Protection Donna Summer](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hBzoIJdPiZI)

**1988:** The Tunnel of Love Express Tour stops in Philadelphia for the first of two nights.<br>

[Watch Video Highlight](https://videopress.com/embed/kJVz376X)

Fan footage of his arrival at sound check plus full show audio (including Bruce's only known cover of "[Brand New Cadillac](../2019-02-07_matr-bruce-springsteen-and-the-wallflowers-brand-new-cadillac/post.md)") below.<br>

**1999:** Bruce and the E Street Band rehearse for their upcoming Reunion Tour at Asbury Park's Convention Hall.<br>

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/main/assets_webp/2021/03/1999-03-08.jpg.webp)

**2008:** Bruce drops by a benefit for the Ranney School at the Eagle Oaks Country Club in Farmingdale, New Jersey and plays "[Glory Days](../2024-06-04_roll-of-the-dice-glory-days/post.md)" with Tim McLoone and the Shirleys. (photo credit: backstreets.com)<br>

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/main/assets_webp/2018/03/2008-0308.jpg.webp)

**2018:** *Springsteen on Broadway* continues its theatrical run at the Walter Kerr Theater in New York City.<br>

**2021:** Bruce and President Obama release the fourth episode of their podcast series, *Renegades: Born in the U.S.A.* This episode is entitled "Born to Run: The Loss of Innocence," in which Bruce and Barack talk about travelling across America and finding home.<br>

[Spotify Embed: Traveling the U.S. & Finding Home](https://open.spotify.com/embed/episode/25YjJeERNhvygT4kXBzY4o?si=80ef3d4930854203&utm_source=oembed)

**2022:** At the Fire Department Building in his hometown of Freehold, Bruce appears at a community event announcing the development of *My Hometown: The Bruce Springsteen Story Center*, slated to open in that space in 2024.<br>

[Watch on Youtube: Bruce Springsteen in Freehold today. March 8th 2022.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L8QCaf28KRU)

[Watch on Youtube: Exhibit honoring music icon Bruce Springsteen to open in Freehold in 2024](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GN5IqJloo4Q)
