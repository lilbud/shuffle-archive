***New for 2025:** Video for 2014*

**1976:** The second of two nights in Cleveland on the Chicken Scratch Tour features the last “[When You Walk in the Room](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/10/08/cover-me-when-you-walk-in-the-room/)” for 33 years.

**1984:** Bruce sits in with Cats on a Smooth Surface at The Stone Pony in Asbury Park for four songs: “Proud Mary,” “[Dirty Water,](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2022/03/18/cover-me-dirty-water/)” “[I’m Bad, I’m Nationwide](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/02/17/cover-me-im-bad-im-nationwide/),” and “[Lucille](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/11/29/cover-me-lucille/).”

[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E0nGuxZTSNo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E0nGuxZTSNo)
