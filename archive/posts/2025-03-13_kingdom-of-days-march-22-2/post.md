**2018:** *Springsteen on Broadway* continues its theatrical run at the Walter Kerr Theater in New York City.<br>

**2021:** Bruce and President Barack Obama release the sixth episode of their podcast, *Renegades: Born in the U.S.A.*. This episode is entitled “Wrestling with Ghosts: American Men.”<br>

**2024:** Bruce and the E Street Band perform in Las Vegas for the first time in over two decades, opening with the tour premiere of “[Roll of the Dice](../2020-01-18_roll-of-the-dice-roll-of-the-dice/post.md)” and kicking off the encores with their first performance of “[Viva Las Vegas](../2018-12-07_cover-me-viva-las-vegas/post.md)” since 2002.<br>
