**1962:** Adele Springsteen takes Bruce and Ginny to their first-ever rock concert when Chubby Checker plays at Steel Pier in Atlantic City. She also takes them to see Anita Bryant that evening. Bruce enjoys the afternoon show a lot more than the evening show.

**1984:** A big, big night for Bruce Springsteen and the E Street Band: The Born in the U.S.A. Tour kicks off at the St. Paul Civic Center Arena, with Nils and Patti making their official debuts as full-fledged members of the band. Film director Brian De Palma is on hand to film Bruce’s performance (twice!) of “Dancing in the Dark” for the now classic music video. This is also Courtney Cox’s debut–as most people know, she’s the “lucky fan” pulled out of the audience to dance with Bruce on-stage.

Watch footage from the video premiere below, and then listen to a never-before-circulated soundboard recording of Bruce’s first-ever public concert performance of “Dancing in the Dark.” (Sorry about those drum machines…)
