It’s pretty tough to do something new with “[I’m on Fire](../2021-09-11_roll-of-the-dice-im-on-fire/post.md)” — it’s very likely the most covered song in Bruce’s catalog.

Dan Patrick Fulton pulls it off, though, by mashing it up with “Sex on Fire” by Kings of Leon–a neat pairing that works both musically and thematically.

[Watch on Youtube: (I'm/Sex) On Fire (Bruce Springsteen Kings Of Leon Mashup Cover) YIS 14](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_Ak4uHuUPK4)

Dan gets bonus points for going all in with full costume dress and some fantastic editing, too.

Thanks, Dan, for providing a superb entry for our new “Spare Parts” mash-up series. (Check out the first entry [here](../2020-04-16_spare-parts-michael-petrella-dancing-in-the-dark-i-wanna-dance-with-somebody/post.md).)
