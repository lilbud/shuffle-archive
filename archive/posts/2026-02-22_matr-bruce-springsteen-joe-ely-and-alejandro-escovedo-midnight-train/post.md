“Midnight Train” is a Joe Ely deep cut from *Wheels of Fortune,* the 2004 comeback album for The Flatlanders, of which Ely was a founding member. 

[Watch on Youtube: Watch Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y_TyiFj-3uw)

The song is rich with the kind of roaming, restless energy and train imagery that makes a Springsteen collaboration feel almost inevitable, despite the track’s relative obscurity.

It happened at SxSW 2012: Bruce joined his Texas buddies Joe Ely and Alejandro Escovedo for The Flatlanders’ “Midnight Train.”
