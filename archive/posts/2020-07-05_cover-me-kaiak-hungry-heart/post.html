Kaiak is a folk/indie/pop team-up of Swedish musicians Emil Gullhamn and Marcos Ubeda.

Emil and Marcos draw the inspiration for their music from nature, friends, and “really good coffee.”

That description lends itself well to their covers, too, because I can totally dig listening to their version of Bruce’s “Hungry Heart” while taking my morning coffee outside on a lazy weekend morning.

Watch on Youtube: Kaiak - Hungry Heart (Acoustic Version)

That cover track has been around for a few years, but recently the guys teased us with a thirty-second video to go with it.

Watch on Youtube: Hungry Heart - Bruce Springsteen (Kaiak acoustic cover)
