You’re familiar with Lisa Lowell’s voice, even if you don’t know her name.

You can hear her backing vocals all over Bruce’s [*Wrecking Ball*](../2023-03-28_album-companion-wrecking-ball/post.md)album, as far back as “[Better Days](../1992-02-02_roll-of-the-dice-better-days/post.md),” and even on recently released tracks like “[I’ll Stand By You](../2018-08-09_roll-of-the-dice-ill-stand-by-you-always/post.md).” And of course, she was an on-stage presence throughout the Seeger Session Tour.

We actually heard a bit of Lisa’s work yesterday in Patti Scialfa’s “[Rainy Day Man](https://estreetshuffle.com/?p=19060).” We rarely get a chance to hear her lead vocals, though. That’s too bad, because she’s a wonderful singer.

So today, let’s take a listen to Lisa performing Bruce’s “[Secret Garden](../2019-05-30_roll-of-the-dice-secret-garden/post.md)” live on E Street Radio way back in 2011. She gives it a terrific and nuanced reading.

[Watch on Youtube: Lisa Lowell: Secret Garden](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9ntnLKCaaLg)

(By the way, there’s another familiar talent on display in that performance, although you can’t actually see him: that’s Larry Eagle of the Sessions Band on percussion.)

For more Lisa Lowell, check out her solo album, *Beautiful Behavior*. We took a look at one of the album’s standout tracks (“[The Moulin Rouge](../2018-12-12_matr-lisa-lowell-and-bruce-springsteen-the-moulin-rouge/post.md)“) a year and a half ago, because Bruce plays guitar on it, but the whole album is great.
