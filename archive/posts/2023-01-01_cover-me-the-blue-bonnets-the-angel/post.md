I don't know much about The Blue Bonnets other than that they contributed to the excellent *For You* Italian tribute album back in 1995.

But their contribution wasn't just another of the countless "[I'm on Fire](../1984-01-08_roll-of-the-dice-im-on-fire/post.md)" or "[Dancing in the Dark](../1984-01-13_roll-of-the-dice-dancing-in-the-dark/post.md)" renditions out there… these guys get serious bonus points for digging *deep* in Bruce's catalog and selecting a song that almost no one ever touches: "[The Angel](../2011-01-06_roll-of-the-dice-the-angel/post.md)," from Bruce's very first album.

Not many fans would rank "[The Angel](../2011-01-06_roll-of-the-dice-the-angel/post.md)" very high in Bruce's catalog, but if Bruce had arranged the song like the Blue Bonnets did, I bet a whole lot more would. Take a listen:

[Watch on Youtube: The Angel](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uygE2fvfL2E)

That's a seriously good arrangement and performance–so much so that it I wish Bruce would give it a go in concert next time around.

If anyone has any other information about this band, I'd love to know.
