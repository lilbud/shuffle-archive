**2023:** Bruce and Patti are scheduled to present (and perform) at the first annual American Music Honors at Monmouth University, but they are unfortunately forced to cancel when they test positive for COVID-19 the morning of the event. Still, Bruce records video tributes for honorees Darlene Love and Steven Van Zandt, and his videos are shown during the event.
