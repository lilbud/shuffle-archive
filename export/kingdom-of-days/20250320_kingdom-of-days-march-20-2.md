**1974:** Bruce and his band play two shows at their third night at Gertie’s in Dallas.
**1977:** “Juicy Brucey” and the E Street Band bring the Lawsuit Tour to Providence College.
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/1977-0320.jpg)

**2000:** The day after the E Street Band plays a reunion show in New Orleans, Bruce and Patti visit Emmylou Harris in her producer’s New Orleans studio, where they contribute backing vocals for Emmylou’s song “[Tragedy](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/01/17/meeting-across-the-river-emmylou-harris-bruce-springsteen-patti-scialfa/).”

**2003:** On the eve of the Iraq War, Bruce opens the Australia leg of The Rising Tour with an acoustic “[Born in the U.S.A.](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/09/21/roll-of-the-dice-born-in-the-u-s-a/)” at the Telstra Dome in Melbourne. “[War](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/05/27/cover-me-war/)” also receives a rare outing.

This show is available as part of Bruce’s official archive series–get it [here](https://live.brucespringsteen.net/live-music/0,27552/Bruce-Springsteen-mp3-flac-download-3-20-2008-Conseco-Fieldhouse-Indianapolis-IN.html).
