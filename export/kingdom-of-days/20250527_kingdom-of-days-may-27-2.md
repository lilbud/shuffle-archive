**1978:** At Night Two in Philadelphia on the Darkness Tour, the core of the set finally gels, as fans hear the first consecutive plays of “[The Promised Land](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/07/04/roll-of-the-dice-the-promised-land/),” “[Prove It All Night](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/10/24/roll-of-the-dice-prove-it-all-night/),” “[Racing in the Street](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2021/10/03/roll-of-the-dice-racing-in-the-street/),” “Thunder Road,” and “Jungleland.” This core five-pack will last throughout the rest of the tour.

Garry joins them for “[Heartbreak Hotel](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2021/04/11/matr-bruce-springsteen-and-robert-gordon-heartbreak-hotel/)” as well.

**1981:** At the Brighton Center on the River Tour, Bruce and the E Street Band perform “[The Price You Pay](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/09/23/roll-of-the-dice-the-price-you-pay/)” for the last time for 28 years.

**1983:** Bruce and the E Street Band record at The Hit Factory in New York City for *Born in the U.S.A.*, although none of the tracks from today’s session will make the album.
**1985:** Bruce and the E Street Band (plus Little Steven, director John Sayles and crew) take over Maxwell’s in Hoboken for the first of two days of filming for the video for “[Glory Days.](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2024/06/04/roll-of-the-dice-glory-days/)” Watch local news coverage below.

**1999:** Bruce and the E Street Band make a Reunion Tour stop at the Flanders Expo in Ghent, where Elliott Murphy guest-stars on “[Hungry Heart](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2022/12/21/roll-of-the-dice-hungry-heart/)” and “Born to Run.”

**2000:** Bruce plays his first-ever show in Las Vegas when the Reunion Tour stops at the MGM Grand Garden Arena. Bruce opens and closes with the premiere of “[Viva Las Vegas](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/12/07/cover-me-viva-las-vegas/),” and he tour premieres “[Can’t Help Falling in Love](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2021/05/06/cover-me-cant-help-falling-in-love/)” for the occasion as well.
