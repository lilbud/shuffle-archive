**1972:** The Bruce Springsteen Band plays at The Back Door in Richmond.
**1974:** Bruce cancels a concert in Cleveland after firing drummer Vini Lopez the week before.
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/The_Plain_Dealer_1974_02_08_84.jpg)
***![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1972-02-18.jpg)* 1975:** Bruce and the E Street Band play a gig at John Carroll University in Cleveland. Tim Russert organized the concert; Tim will later go on to host *Meet the Press* for many years.
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/1975-02-18-204x300.jpg?resize=758%2C1114)

**1984:** Bruce and Nils jam at Bruce’s house and listen to the just-completed *Born in the U.S.A.* album. At some point, they watch TV and see MTV announce Steve Van Zandt’s departure from the band. Nils puts in a good word for himself that will pay off not that far down the road.
**1993:** *MTV Plugged* (the video release) is certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of America.
