# February 15
#### Originally Published on Ken Rosen's E Street Shuffle blog at https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2025/02/15/kingdom-of-days-february-15-2/
#### Published: February 15, 2025
#### Last Updated: November 24, 2025
![melbourne14.jpg](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/02/melbourne14.jpg)

![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1981-02-15a.png)
**1974:** After firing drummer Vini Lopez three days earlier, Bruce cancels a show at the University of Toledo so that the band has time to rehearse with new drummer Ernest “Boom” Carter.
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/1974-02-15-149x300.jpg?resize=758%2C1526)
**1977:** Bruce and the E Street band return to the U.S. and continue the Lawsuit Tour at Detroit’s Masonic Temple Theater, where “[Detroit Medley](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/12/27/cover-me-detroit-medley/)” makes its tour premiere.
[Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - DETROIT MEDLEY  1977 (audio)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kiO9Y-V2z8Y)
![](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kiO9Y-V2z8Y)
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/46881241794_6812cfd98f_o-300x178.jpg?resize=758%2C450)
**1981**: Bruce and band take the River Tour to the Civic Center in Lakeland, Florida.

![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1981-02-15b.png)
**1983:** In the studio, Bruce records “[Seven Tears](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/03/17/roll-of-the-dice-seven-tears/)” and “The Money We Didn’t Make.” The former circulates among collectors, the latter has not yet surfaced.
[Youtube: BRUCE SPRINGSTEEN - Seven Tears (outtake, '83)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6DNs1enmtNM)
![](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6DNs1enmtNM)
**1988:** At the Wonder Bar in Asbury Park, Bruce films footage used in the video for “[One Step Up](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/08/17/roll-of-the-dice-one-step-up/).”
[Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - One Step Up (Official Video)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MkFQHScyti0)
![](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MkFQHScyti0)
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1988-02-15.png)
**1995:** Bruce joins Melissa Etheridge for her MTV Unplugged performance in Brooklyn. They perform a gorgeous duet of “Thunder Road” together.
[Youtube: Bruce Springsteen and Melissa Etheridge - Thunder Road](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5cXzFhZVMnQ)
![](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5cXzFhZVMnQ)
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/1995-02-15.jpg)
**1996:** Bruce plays his third solo acoustic show in Germany, this time in Munich.
[https://videopress.com/embed/3d7UJujn?hd=1&amp;cover=1&amp;loop=0&amp;autoPlay=0&amp;permalink=1&amp;muted=0&amp;controls=1&amp;playsinline=0&amp;useAverageColor=0&amp;preloadContent=metadata](https://videopress.com/embed/3d7UJujn?hd=1&amp;cover=1&amp;loop=0&amp;autoPlay=0&amp;permalink=1&amp;muted=0&amp;controls=1&amp;playsinline=0&amp;useAverageColor=0&amp;preloadContent=metadata)
**1997:** Moving on to Melbourne, Bruce performs solo at the Palais Theatre, where he plays “[4th of July, Asbury Park (Sandy)](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/04/26/roll-of-the-dice-4th-of-july-asbury-park-sandy/)” for the first time in Australia.
[https://videopress.com/embed/qmr0Nwtj?hd=1&amp;cover=1&amp;loop=0&amp;autoPlay=0&amp;permalink=1&amp;muted=0&amp;controls=1&amp;playsinline=0&amp;useAverageColor=0&amp;preloadContent=metadata](https://videopress.com/embed/qmr0Nwtj?hd=1&amp;cover=1&amp;loop=0&amp;autoPlay=0&amp;permalink=1&amp;muted=0&amp;controls=1&amp;playsinline=0&amp;useAverageColor=0&amp;preloadContent=metadata)
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1997-02-15.png)
**2014:** The first of two nights in Melbourne features a surprise guest in the opening numbers: Eddie Vedder joins the E Street Band for “Highway to Hell” and “[Darkness on the Edge of Town](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/04/10/roll-of-the-dice-darkness-on-the-edge-of-town/).” Another surprise shortly follows: a full performance of the *Born in the U.S.A.* album. Also tour premiering tonight: “[Jole Blon,](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/08/29/matr-gary-u-s-bonds-and-bruce-springsteen-jole-blon/)” “[My Hometown](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/04/09/roll-of-the-dice-my-hometown/),” “[I’m Goin’ Down](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2021/06/05/roll-of-the-dice-im-goin-down/),” and “[Factory](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/10/31/roll-of-the-dice-factory/).”
