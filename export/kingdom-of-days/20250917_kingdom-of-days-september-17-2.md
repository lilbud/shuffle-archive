**1966:** The Castiles play the Surf ‘n’ See Club in Sea Bright, New Jersey.
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/1966-0917-300x257.jpg?resize=758%2C649)
**1971:** The Bruce Springsteen Band is in residence at the Student Prince in Asbury Park.
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/1971-0917-231x300.jpg?resize=758%2C984)
**1975:** *Born to Run* hits #1 on the charts; Bruce and the boys celebrate at their tour stop in Oklahoma City, their first-ever show in that state.
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/1975-09-17-300x183.jpg?resize=739%2C451)

**1978:** Bruce and the E Street Band close out their three-night Darkness Tour stand at the Palladium in New York City, tour premiering “[Meeting Across the River](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/08/04/roll-of-the-dice-meeting-across-the-river/).”

Tour stops at Montreal’s Olympic Stadium, where Bruce makes a guest-appearance during Sting’s set, duetting on “[Every Breath You Take](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/04/07/matr-sting-and-bruce-springsteen-every-breath-you-take/),” Bruce’s first performance of that song.
