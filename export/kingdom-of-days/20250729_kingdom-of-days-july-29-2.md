# Kingdom of Days: July 29
#### Originally Published on Ken Rosen's E Street Shuffle blog at https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2025/07/29/kingdom-of-days-july-29-2/
#### Published: July 29, 2025
#### Last Updated: July 18, 2025
![patti.jpg](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/patti.jpg)

***New for 2025:** Audio for 1999*
**1953:** Singer/songwriter and First Lady of E Street Vivienne Patricia Scialfa is born on this day in Deal, New Jersey. Happy birthday, Patti!
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/patti-300x261.jpg?resize=745%2C648)
**1966:** The Castiles headline at the Loew’s 35 Shore Drive-in Theater’s 10th anniversary party in Hazlet, New Jersey.
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/1966-07-29a.png)
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/07/1966-07-29b-258x300.png?resize=758%2C881)
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/1966-0729.gif?resize=742%2C431)
**1971:** The Bruce Springsteen Band plays two shows at D’Scene in South Amboy, New Jersey. The setlist includes “[Natural Magic,](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/05/19/roll-of-the-dice-natural-magic/)” “[Goin’ Back to Georgia](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/03/07/roll-of-the-dice-goin-back-to-georgia/),” “[Dance Dance Dance](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/06/22/roll-of-the-dice-dance-dance-dance/),” and more.

![](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ok630L5Z9D8)
**1975:** Before their gig that evening at the Carter Barron Amphitheater in Washington, DC’s Rock Creek Park, Bruce stops by a local radio station for an on-air interview.

**1976:** Mike Appel countersues Bruce for breach of contract, escalating their legal battle.
**1978:** At the Bayfront Center Arena in St. Petersburg, Bruce opens his Darkness Tour show with his debut of “[Oh Boy](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/05/17/cover-me-oh-boy/)” and adds a second cover debut later in the show: Chuck Berry’s “[Around and Around](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/02/26/cover-me-around-and-around/).”

**1987:** Bruce makes a guest appearance with Jah Love at Key Largo in Belmar, New Jersey.
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/07/1987-07-29-281x300.png?resize=758%2C809)
**1999:** At Night Seven of the opening fifteen-night stand of the U.S. Reunion Tour in the Meadowlands, Bruce opens with the first “[The Ties That Bind](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/03/09/roll-of-the-dice-the-ties-that-bind/)” in 18 years and tour debuts (officially) a corny and charming “[Give the Girl a Kiss](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/06/17/roll-of-the-dice-give-the-girl-a-kiss/)” for Patti’s birthday. Bobby Bandiera makes an encore guest appearance on “[Hungry Heart.](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2022/12/21/roll-of-the-dice-hungry-heart/)”
