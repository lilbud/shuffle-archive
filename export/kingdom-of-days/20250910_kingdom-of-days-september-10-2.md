# Kingdom of Days: September 10
#### Originally Published on Ken Rosen's E Street Shuffle blog at https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2025/09/10/kingdom-of-days-september-10-2/
#### Published: September 10, 2025
#### Last Updated: August 31, 2025
![19880910_2_front.jpg](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/19880910_2_front.jpg)

***New for 2025:** New entry for 1979; audio for 2003*
**1971:** The Bruce Springsteen Band continues their residency at The Student Prince.
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/1971-0910-300x169.jpg?resize=758%2C426)
**1978:** Bruce and the E Street Band bring the Darkness Tour to the Riverfront Coliseum in Cincinnati, where “[Detroit Medley](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/12/27/cover-me-detroit-medley/)” makes its tour premiere. If you can’t make the show, you can listen to a two-hour radio special on Bruce premiering tonight on the King Biscuit Flower Hour.
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/1978-09-10a-300x162.jpg?resize=758%2C409)
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/1978-09-10b.jpg)
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/1978-0910-144x300.jpg?resize=758%2C1579)
**1979:** Bruce records “Mr. Outside” on a cassette recorder at home in Holmdel. He will release it 36 years later on *The Ties That Bind: The River Collection*.
[Youtube: Mr. Outside (Studio Outtake - 1980)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PLa8TaCNM28)
![](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PLa8TaCNM28)
**1981:** At Night Two on the River Tour at Chicago’s Rosemont Horizon, Bruce and the E Street Band play the one and only tour performance of “[She’s the One](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2021/04/04/roll-of-the-dice-shes-the-one/),” its last appearance for almost seven years.
[Youtube: Bruce Springsteen - MONA  SHE'S THE ONE 1981 (audio)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zHxCBO7yzCI)
![](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zHxCBO7yzCI)
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/1981-09-10-300x84.jpg?resize=758%2C212)
**1984:** On a free day in between shows, Clarence appears as a guest on *Late Night with David Letterman,* and he performs a familiar song with the Red Bank Rockers.

**2003:** One final Canadian show on the Rising Tour at the Skydome in Toronto, where Bruce pays tribute to Warren Zevon (who died three days prior) by opening with his only performance to date of “[My Ride’s Here](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2022/03/24/cover-me-my-rides-here/).”

**2007:** Bruce and the E Street Band begin rehearsing for their upcoming Magic Tour at Convention Hall in Asbury Park.
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/2007-09-10-300x270.jpg?resize=758%2C682)
