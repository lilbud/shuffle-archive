# Kingdom of Days: June 7
#### Originally Published on Ken Rosen's E Street Shuffle blog at https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2025/06/07/kingdom-of-days-june-7-2/
#### Published: June 07, 2025
#### Last Updated: June 01, 2025
![20090607_3_front.jpg](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/20090607_3_front.jpg)

***New for 2025:** Audio for 1972; review for 1973; new entry for 1977*
**1969:** Child headlines at the eighth grade graduation concert at Blessed Sacrament Regional School in Margate, New Jersey.
**1972:** Recording sessions begin for Bruce’s first album, *[Greetings From Asbury Park, N.J.](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2023/01/05/album-companion-greetings-from-asbury-park-n-j/)* Bruce records five solo tracks today: “[Arabian Nights,](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2021/03/23/roll-of-the-dice-arabian-nights/)” “[Cowboys of the Sea](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2021/09/17/roll-of-the-dice-cowboys-of-the-sea/),” “[Growin’ Up,](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2022/09/29/roll-of-the-dice-growin-up/)” “[Lady and the Doctor](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/09/08/roll-of-the-dice-lady-and-the-doctor/),” and “[Street Queen](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/06/19/roll-of-the-dice-street-queen/).” Only “Growin’ Up” will eventually be released, and even that song will be re-recorded later in the month before being selected for the album.
[https://videopress.com/embed/IvoYACpv?hd=0&amp;cover=1](https://videopress.com/embed/IvoYACpv?hd=0&amp;cover=1)
**1973:** Bruce and the E Street Band open for Chicago at the Onondaga County War Memorial Auditorium in Syracuse, New York. The local paper mis-identifies Bruce, and they aren’t even close: somehow, they refer to him as “Bris Christy.” Mr. Reschke didn’t do so well with the song titles, either. See if you can figure out the songs Bruce actually played below.
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/Syracuse_Herald_Journal_1973_06_08_16.jpg?w=758&#038;ssl=1)
**1976:** Bruce and most of the E Street Band join Southside Johnny and The Asbury Jukes on stage at a private party for *Crawdaddy* magazine.
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/1976-0612.jpg)
**1977:** Bruce and the band work on “[Don’t Look Back](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/05/03/roll-of-the-dice-dont-look-back/)” at Atlantic Studios in New York City.
**1981:** In Birmingham, Pete Townshend makes a guest appearance in the encores of the first show of the last European stand of the River Tour. In the main set, Bruce performs “[Drive All Night](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/02/27/roll-of-the-dice-drive-all-night/)” for the last time for 24 years.
