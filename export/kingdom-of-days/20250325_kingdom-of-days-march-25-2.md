#### Last Updated: March 19, 2025

***New for 2025:** Expanded entry and audio for 1979; video for 2023; new entry for 2024*
**1976:** The Chicken Scratch Tour kicks off with a show at the Township Auditorium in Columbia, South Carolina–Bruce’s first time performing in the state.
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/The_Columbia_Record_1976_03_26_page_6.jpg)
**1977:** The Lawsuit Tour comes to a close with a final show at Boston’s Music Hall, featuring the last “[Don’t Look Back](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/05/03/roll-of-the-dice-dont-look-back/)” for 22 years, the last “[(Your Love Keeps Lifting Me) Higher and Higher](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/09/23/cover-me-your-love-keeps-lifting-me-higher-and-higher/)” for 32 years, and the last “[Action in the Streets](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2022/06/27/roll-of-the-dice-action-in-the-streets/)” for 34 years.

**1979:** At his home studio in Holmdel, Bruce and the E Street Band rehearse “[Meet Me in the City](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/08/23/roll-of-the-dice-meet-me-in-the-city/).”
[Youtube: Bruce Springsteen In The City Tonight #4 aka Meet Me in the City Tonight](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-KuFJEzoFuE)
![](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-KuFJEzoFuE)
That evening, Bruce catches The Ramones playing at The Fast Lane in Asbury Park. Backstage, Joey Ramone asks Bruce to write a song for them. Bruce starts writing “[Hungry Heart](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2022/12/21/roll-of-the-dice-hungry-heart/)” that very night.
**1984:** Bruce joins Cats on a Smooth Surface on-stage at The Stone Pony in Asbury Park for “[I’m Bad, I’m Nationwide](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2020/02/17/cover-me-im-bad-im-nationwide/)” and “[Lucille](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/11/29/cover-me-lucille/).”
**1996:** Bruce performs “[Dead Man Walkin’](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/07/24/roll-of-the-dice-dead-man-walkin/)” at the Academy Awards, where it is nominated (but loses) for Best Original Song. Watch the CNN news coverage and full performance:
