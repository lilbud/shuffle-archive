# Kingdom of Days: October 30
#### Originally Published on Ken Rosen's E Street Shuffle blog at https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2025/10/30/kingdom-of-days-october-30-2/
#### Published: October 30, 2025
#### Last Updated: October 10, 2025
![19761030_2_front.jpg](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/19761030_2_front.jpg)

***New for 2025:** No updates*
**1965:** Castiles drummer Bart Haynes quits the band. A year later, Bart will join the Marines and ship out to Vietnam, where he will be killed in action. Bruce will remember Bart often–in his shows, in print, and on Broadway.
[Youtube: Born In the U.S.A. (Introduction Part 2) (Springsteen on Broadway - Official Audio)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p53I93UK9j0)
![](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p53I93UK9j0)
**1971:** The Bruce Springsteen Band opens for Cactus at Virginia Commonwealth University in Richmond.

**1973:** Bruce and the boys headline two shows at The Main Point in Bryn Mawr, Pennsylvania–their second of a three-night stand. Richard Blackwell makes a guest appearance on congas.
**1976:** Patti Smith guest-stars on Night Three (of six) at the Palladium in New York City, joining Bruce and the Band for an unusual version of “Rosalita (Come Out Tonight).”

**1980:** Bruce and the E Street Band kick off a four-night stand at the Los Angeles Sports Arena, where Flo and Eddie contribute guest backing vocals to “[Hungry Heart](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2022/12/21/roll-of-the-dice-hungry-heart/).”

**1984:** Bruce releases the latest single, the [title track](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/09/21/roll-of-the-dice-born-in-the-u-s-a/) from his mega-hit *Born in the U.S.A.* album.

![](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tRx212PUa4g)
**1987:** Bruce premieres the video for his latest single, “[Brilliant Disguise](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/02/06/roll-of-the-dice-brilliant-disguise/),” the first single from *[Tunnel of Love](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2022/08/08/album-companion-tunnel-of-love/).* Watch the video and news coverage below.

**2005:** Bruce’s solo acoustic show at the TD Banknorth Garden in Boston is filmed but will remain unreleased with one exception: a video for “[Matamoros Banks](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/10/27/roll-of-the-dice-matamoros-banks/)” that Bruce will release fourteen years later in support of Project Adelante. In the encores, Bruce tour premieres “[Dirty Water](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2022/03/18/cover-me-dirty-water/)” as a Boston special. In attendance tonight: Senator John Kerry.

**2007:** At Night Two in Los Angeles on the Magic Tour, Bruce celebrates Halloween a day early, once again (as in Halloweens past) emerging from a coffin to start the show. Bruce also tour premieres “[Kitty’s Back](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2022/01/22/roll-of-the-dice-kittys-back/)” during the encores.

**2014:** Bruce is interviewed for the [*New York Times* book section](https://www.nytimes.com/2014/11/02/books/review/bruce-springsteen-by-the-book.html), where he provides his reading recommendations.
