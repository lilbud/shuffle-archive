**2012:** The Wrecking Ball Tour starts a Scandinavian swing with a show at Valle Hovin Stadion in Oslo. Bruce opens with an acoustic “[The Promised Land](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/07/04/roll-of-the-dice-the-promised-land/),” tour premieres “[Ain’t Good Enough for You](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/06/02/roll-of-the-dice-aint-good-enough-for-you/)” and “[Streets of Fire,](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2021/11/06/roll-of-the-dice-streets-of-fire/)” and ends the show with a surprise bonus encore of “[Night](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/10/31/roll-of-the-dice-night/).”
