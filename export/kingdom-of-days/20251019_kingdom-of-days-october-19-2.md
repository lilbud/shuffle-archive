**2009:** Bruce performs his penultimate show at the Philadelphia Spectrum, the third of four nights in this final stand. Bruce opens with a shocker–“[When You Walk in the Room](http://estreetshuffle.com/?p=21789),” played here for the first time in 33 years. The setlist also features the first full-band “[I Wanna Marry You](https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2022/04/22/roll-of-the-dice-i-wanna-marry-you/)” in 27 years and the on-stage debut of “Philly Elvis” (a.k.a Nick Ferraro) for the tour premiere of “[All Shook Up](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/08/03/matr-bruce-springsteen-and-the-philly-elvis-all-shook-up/).” Oh and not for nothing, the show also includes a full album performance of *Born to Run*. For more highlights from this show, see my [*Where the Band Was* report.](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/10/19/where-the-band-was-philadelphia-october-19-2009/)
