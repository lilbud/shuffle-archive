![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/12/1967-1227-260x300.jpg?resize=758%2C874)
**1968:** Earth plays four sets at Le Teendezvous in New Shrewsbury.
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/12/1968-1227.jpg?resize=758%2C1083)
**1973:** Bruce and the E Street Band kick off a four-night/two-shows-nightly stand at The Main Point in Bryn Mawr, Pennsylvania.
![](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/12/1973-1227-300x192.jpg?resize=758%2C485)
**1975:** Bruce and the band play their first of a four-night year-closing stand at the Tower Theater in Philadelphia. Tonight sees the tour premiere of “[Back in the U.S.A.](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2019/03/11/cover-me-back-in-the-u-s-a/)” and the last full-band “[Lost in the Flood](http://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/02/20/roll-of-the-dice-lost-in-the-flood/)” for 25 years.
