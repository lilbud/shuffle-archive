# Meeting Across the River: Bruce Springsteen and Jon Bon Jovi, It's My Life
#### Originally Published on Ken Rosen's E Street Shuffle blog at https://estreetshuffle.com/index.php/2018/06/13/meeting-across-the-river-bruce-springsteen-and-jon-bon-jovi-its-my-life/

#### Last Updated: April 14, 2019
![mylife-e1528501806892.jpg](https://estreetshuffle.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/mylife-e1528501806892.jpg)

A one-time-only performance: Jon Bon Jovi makes a guest appearance at Bruce’s 2003 holiday show in Asbury Park, and the two perform Bon Jovi’s “It’s My Life” together.
[Youtube: Jon Bon Jovi and Bruce Springsteen - It's my life](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=a8A8IDk_Hd8)
![](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=a8A8IDk_Hd8)
Bon Jovi’s original 2000 single peaked at #33 in the U.S., but it topped the charts in other countries and re-established the band for the new century.
